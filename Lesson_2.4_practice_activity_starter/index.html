Michael Jordan

Biography

Michael Jeffrey Jordan, born on February 17, 1963, in Brooklyn, New York, is widely regarded as one of the greatest basketball players of all time. Jordan played the majority of his career with the Chicago Bulls in the NBA and achieved numerous accolades and records throughout his career. His combination of skill, athleticism, and competitiveness made him an iconic figure in the world of sports.
